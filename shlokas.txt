
1.1॥
धृतराष्ट्र उवाच |
धर्मक्षेत्रे कुरुक्षेत्रे समवेता युयुत्सवः |
मामकाः पाण्डवाश्चैव किमकुर्वत सञ्जय ॥
Translation:
Dhritarashtra said: O Sanjaya, assembled at the holy land of Kurukshetra and desiring battle, what did my sons and the sons of Pandu do?
